# Growth Tracking Feature - User Guide

## Overview
The growth tracking feature allows parents to monitor their infant's growth over time by recording and visualizing height, weight, and head circumference measurements. This guide will walk you through how to use this feature effectively.

## Accessing the Growth Tracking Feature

1. **Log in** to your FirstSteps account
2. **Select an infant** from your dashboard or create a new infant profile
3. **Click on "Growth"** in the left sidebar navigation
4. You will be taken to the growth tracking page for the selected infant

## Understanding the Growth Tracking Page

### Charts Section
The growth tracking page displays three interactive charts:
- **Height Tracking Chart**: Shows your infant's height measurements over time
- **Weight Tracking Chart**: Shows your infant's weight measurements over time
- **Head Circumference Tracking Chart**: Shows your infant's head circumference measurements over time

Each chart includes:
- **Blue Line**: Your infant's actual measurements
- **Red Dashed Lines**: Minimum and maximum target values based on WHO growth standards
- **Red Dot (if present)**: Birth measurement (automatically included if available in the infant profile)

### Timeline Section
Below the charts, you'll see a timeline table that lists all measurements in chronological order:
- **Date**: When the measurement was taken
- **Age**: The infant's age at the time of measurement
- **Height**: Height measurement in centimeters
- **Weight**: Weight measurement in kilograms
- **Head Circumference**: Head circumference measurement in centimeters

Birth measurements (if available) are highlighted with a light red background.

## Adding New Measurements

1. **Click the "Add Measurement" button** at the top right of the page
2. **Fill in the measurement details**:
   - **Date**: When the measurement was taken (defaults to today)
   - **Height**: Height in centimeters (optional)
   - **Weight**: Weight in kilograms (optional)
   - **Head Circumference**: Head circumference in centimeters (optional)
   - **Notes**: Any additional information about the measurement (optional)
3. **Click "Add Measurement"** to save the new measurement
4. The charts and timeline will automatically update to include your new measurement

## Viewing Birth Measurements

If you've entered birth length and weight in your infant's profile:
1. These values will automatically appear as the first data point in the charts
2. They will be clearly labeled as "Birth" in the chart labels
3. They will appear in the timeline table with a light red background
4. They will be displayed with special red dots on the charts

## Interpreting the Charts

### Growth Trends
- **Blue Line**: Follows your infant's actual measurements over time
- **Upward Trend**: Indicates healthy growth
- **Flat or Downward Trend**: May indicate a need to consult with a healthcare provider

### Target Ranges
- **Red Dashed Lines**: Show the normal range based on WHO growth standards
- **Within Range**: If the blue line stays between the red dashed lines, growth is considered normal
- **Outside Range**: If the blue line goes significantly above or below the red dashed lines, consult with a healthcare provider

## Best Practices

### Measurement Frequency
- **Newborns to 3 months**: Weekly measurements
- **3 to 6 months**: Bi-weekly measurements
- **6 to 12 months**: Monthly measurements
- **12+ months**: Monthly or bi-monthly measurements

### Recording Measurements
1. Always record measurements on the same day they're taken
2. Use consistent units (centimeters for height and head circumference, kilograms for weight)
3. Add notes if there were any special circumstances during measurement
4. Record measurements at the same time of day when possible

### Using the Data
1. Share the charts with your pediatrician during checkups
2. Look for consistent growth patterns rather than individual measurements
3. Pay attention to sudden changes in growth patterns
4. Use the timeline to track measurements over time

## Troubleshooting

### Charts Not Displaying
- Ensure you have added at least one measurement
- Refresh the page if charts don't appear
- Check that JavaScript is enabled in your browser

### Missing Birth Measurements
- Verify that birth length and weight are entered in the infant profile
- If birth measurements were not recorded, they won't appear in the charts

### Data Entry Issues
- All measurements must be positive numbers
- Height typically ranges from 45-100 cm for infants
- Weight typically ranges from 2-15 kg for infants
- Head circumference typically ranges from 30-50 cm for infants

## Privacy and Security

- All growth measurements are stored securely in your account
- Only you and authorized family members can view this data
- Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
- You can delete any measurement at any time

## Getting Help

The growth tracking feature provides a powerful tool for monitoring your infant's development. By consistently recording measurements and reviewing the charts, you can ensure your infant is growing healthily and seek appropriate care when needed.
